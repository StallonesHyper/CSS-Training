About Us:


We Are A PADI 5 Star Dive Center – 36314 Operating From Calangute, North Goa Since 2006.
The Diving Season In Goa Is From Mid Of October To End Of April. Visibility Around Goa Is Normally 5-10 m Due To The Two Rivers, The Mandovi And Zuari Meeting The Sea. The Dive Sites Are Mostly Shallow Around Grande Island 5-15 M And The Water Temperature Is Around 28°C. At Goa Aquatics We Love To Introduce You To The Beautiful Underwater World.
